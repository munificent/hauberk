Quick Reference

Movement

There are two sets of direction keys:

I O P
K   ;
, . /
 7 8 9
4   6
1 2 3

They can be combined with modifier keys like so ("alt" is "option" on Mac):

DirectionWalk one step in direction, attack adjacent monster
Shift-DirectionRun in direction until disturbed
Alt-DirectionUse last skill in that direction

Other commands

L, Numpad-5, EnterRest one turn, select menu item
Shift-L, Shift-Numpad-5Rest until fully healed or disturbed
CClose door
UUse or equip item
DDrop item
TThrow item
XSwap—re-equip last unequipped item
SSelect a skill to use
Shift-SOpen skill screen
AOpen hero info screen
QExit level after finishing quest
Shift-FForfeit level
